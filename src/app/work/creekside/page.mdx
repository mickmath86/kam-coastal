## Overview

KAM Coastal led the revitalization of an expansive senior living apartment community in Riverside, California, on behalf of long-term client **Village Investments**.  
This $6 million project bridged the gap from the 1990s into the 2020s, delivering a modernized living environment and long-term asset preservation.

The scope included enclosing private patios with new gabled rooflines, installing new 30-year roofing across all 19 buildings, remodeling clubhouse interiors for improved finishes and functionality, and enhancing community spaces with a new barbecue area by the pool and a dedicated dog park.

## The Results

By the end of 2024, KAM Coastal delivered the project on time and within budget.  
The modernization transformed the property’s appeal, increasing resident satisfaction through updated interiors, energy-efficient roofing, and new shared amenities that encourage community interaction.

For Village Investments, the renovation extended the property’s usable life, reduced long-term maintenance liabilities, and positioned the community as one of the most desirable senior living environments in Riverside.  
The upgrades also strengthened leasing competitiveness and preserved long-term asset value for investors, creating both social and financial returns.
